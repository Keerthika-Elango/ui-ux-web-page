MOBILES


The Vivo V9 is one of the first Android phones to sport a notch at the top of the display. It has a 6.3-inch IPS display with a 19:9 ratio and a resolution of 1080x2280 pixels. Powering the phone is a Snapdragon 626 octa-core processor clocked at 2.2GHz. It has 4GB of RAM and the device has 64GB of internal storage. The V9 is a dual SIM device and has two nano SIM slots along with a dedicated microSD card slot. It sports a dual camera setup at the back comprising of a 16-megapixel and a 5-megapixel sensor. It packs in a 3260mAh battery and has a 24-megapixel selfie camera.
